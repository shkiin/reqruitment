== Description ==

Looking for the ultimate solution to streamline your job board website on WordPress? Look no further than Joblook, the best Job Board WordPress Theme meticulously crafted with WP Job Manager integration. Joblook offers unparalleled functionality and versatility, empowering you to effortlessly create a professional platform for job listings. With its sleek design and user-friendly interface, Joblook ensures an exceptional browsing experience for both employers and job seekers alike. From advanced search options to customizable job listing layouts, Joblook caters to your every need, making it the ideal choice for anyone seeking to establish a robust online job portal. Discover the endless possibilities with Joblook and take your job board website to new heights of success.

== Installation ==

1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance > Themes and click the Add New button.
2. Click Upload Theme and Choose File, then select the theme's .zip file. Click Install Now.
3. Click Activate to use your new theme right away.
